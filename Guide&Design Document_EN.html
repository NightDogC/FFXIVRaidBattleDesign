Guide & Description

0.To who have never played FFXIV...

  • This battle is designed based on a MMORPG game Final Fantasy XIV(FFXIV). Like most raid battles in MMORPG, it requires players play roles as tank, DPS and healer, handling mechanics by moving and using skills properly, collaborating each other to defeat the boss. To be more specific, tank should attract enemies' attention and endure damage; DPS should maximize their DPS to damage the Boss; healer should keep the team alive.

  • The design of raid battle in FFXIV tends to have fixed timeline with puzzle solving and low fault tolerance. In other words, the boss will cast specific skills at specific time. Random content exsits but just a little. This means the battle mainly test the recitation of process. Duty will be completed as long as players keep practicing.

  • This raid battle design contains random content to some extent. Handle it carefully please.

1.Basic Instruction

  1. Click Start/Pause to play after choosing a role. Use arrow keys or WASD to move. Use 1/2/3 or J/K/L keys to cast skills.

  2. Roles unchosen will automatically play.

  3. Player will be immediately revived after HP decrease to 0. All players' status, including HP, death count and buffs/debuffs are displayed on the upper left. The current player's buffs/debuffs are also displayed on the lower right.

  4. The battle field is a circle with a radius of 20 meters. Players cannot cross the boundary of the field and will not die if they touch the boundary.

  5. In this document, all percentage player damage introduced is based on DPS and healer's HP. Tanks have additional HP and damage reduction. There is a 5% fluctuation in damage, and 100% HP damage does not necessarily mean death.

  6. Currently only Ranged DPS 2 can be controlled. The way to maximize DPS is using skills by 1-1-1-3-2 first and then continuously loop 1-1-3-2.
